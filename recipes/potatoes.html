Potatoes

Description

A versatile and easy side dish that the whole family will love!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 232 degrees C
  2. Line baking sheet with parchment paper
  3. Place diced potatoes in large bowl with olive oil, salt, pepper, and garlic; toss until well-coated
  4. Transfer to baking sheet and spread out into an even layer
  5. Roast for 20 minutes
  6. Flip and roast for an additional 20 minutes or until brown and crisp (may need to increase cooking time depending on oven)
  7. Remove from oven and toss with chopped parsley
  8. Serve warm
